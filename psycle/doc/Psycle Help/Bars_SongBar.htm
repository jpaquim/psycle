The Song Bar

Click on the areas above to find information about their function.
If you want to increase or decrease the BPM by 10, you can press the CTRL key while you click the button

When using the extra ticks per beat (see song properties), it displays the real BPM and the extra ticks

The VU Meter displays the left and right master volume during playback. There are two bar colours. One indicates RMS and the other peak amplitude. When the peak amplitude goes above 0 dB, the box on the right will turn red to indicate that digital clipping has occurred (and that the volume needs to be lowered).
