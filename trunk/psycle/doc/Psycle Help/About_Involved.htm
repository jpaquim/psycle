Become Involved

If you're interested in helping with Psycle development, contact the developers at Psycledelics. The official Psycle development site (with source code, bug tracker etc) is

http://psycle.sourceforge.net and http://sourceforge.net/projects/psycle

You can (besides working on Psycle itself) also develop native plugins for Psycle.

There are plenty of other ways to be involved in Psycle. You can contribute to the wiki

http://psycle.sourceforge.net/wiki or http://psycle.wikia.com/wiki/Main_Page

or participate in the Psycledelics forum

http://psycle.pastnotecut.org

or in Psycle's Google+ community
