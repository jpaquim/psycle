Playback

Play from Start – Begin playback from the first position in the sequencer.

Play – Begin playback from the beginning of the currently position in the sequencer.

Play Selected Patterns – Play one or more patterns selected in the sequencer.

Stop – Stop all playback.

AutoStop Machines (Playback > AutoStop machines) – All machines not producing any output will be automatically turned off (and on again). This can significantly reduce real-time CPU usage. Currently this causes problems with certain delay-type machines: the machine is turned off before it has completed the delay, so the delay does not continue as long as it should. This error will not appear in the rendered version of your song.

Loop Playback (Playback > Loop playback) – Song playback will be looped continuously.

Enable Audio (Configuration > Enable Audio) – This option allows to free the soundcard driver without closing Psycle and without changing the driver to Silent.
